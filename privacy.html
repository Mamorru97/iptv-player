Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

IPTV Player ("we", "our", or "us") operates the IPTV Player mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as name, email address, or account data.

However, the app may collect limited non-personal data for analytics purposes.

  • App usage data (e.g. screens visited, interactions)
  • Device information (e.g. device type, OS version)
  • Approximate location (country level)

This data is used to improve the app and user experience.

Any user data such as playlists, cached playlist data, and app preferences is stored locally on the user's device only and is not transmitted to us.

2. Log Data

We may collect limited log data automatically through analytics services. This may include device information, usage statistics, and diagnostic data.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The app does not use cookies. However, third-party analytics services may use similar technologies to collect usage data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

The app uses Google Firebase Analytics, which helps us understand how users interact with the app.

https://policies.google.com/privacy

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
